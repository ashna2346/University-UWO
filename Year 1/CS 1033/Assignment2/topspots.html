Top Spots     

My favourite spots for others to discover on campus are:
         

Favourite spot to Study: Allyn and Betty Taylor Library

The library provides a safe, quiet and at the same time a lively environment to focus on study. It even allows students to borrow books for reasearch purposes and print important documents.

 

  
         
 

Favourite spot to Workout: Western Student Recreational Centre(WSRC) 

The recreational centre offers various facilities like aquatic, fitness, gymnasia and sports programs like badminton, equestrian, fencing, judo, karate, squash and others. Besides these facilties, it also houses a large cardio, weight and stretching space.
         

Favourite spot for a Casual Dining: The Wave

It offers a vast menu ranging from fresh pastas to entree salads. It even hosts USC club events and so the place always remains zippy-zappy.
