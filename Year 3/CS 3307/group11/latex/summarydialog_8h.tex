The \doxylink{class_summary_dialog}{Summary\+Dialog} class extends QDialog to provide a modal dialog window that displays a summary of the session\textquotesingle{}s results or statistics. It is designed for use in applications that require end-\/of-\/session reporting, such as quizzes or tasks.
